### Compost Guidelines

Please keep invasives out of the compost. Now is a great time to remove any sprouting Star of Bethleham. 

The six worst invasive offenders are:

* Spider Wort. 
* Star of Bethlehem. 
* Swallow Wort. 
* Garlic Mustard. 
* Gout Weed.
* Field Bindweed.
